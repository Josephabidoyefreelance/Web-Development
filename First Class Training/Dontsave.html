The Lantern's Light

In a small village nestled between rolling hills, there was an old man named Eli, known for carrying a single lantern wherever he went. The lantern wasn’t ordinary; it glowed with a soft, golden light, even when the sun was bright in the sky. People would often ask him why he carried it, but Eli would only smile and say, “It guides me when I can’t see.”
